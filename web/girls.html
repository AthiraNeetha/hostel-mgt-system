HOSTEL FOR GIRLS

There are 4 blocks allotted for Women students of capacity varying from 250 to 700 in each block. The total capacity for Women’s hostel is about 2500.

BLOCK A

This is a fully furnished Girls hostel. This residential facility offers a comfortable accommodation to more than 800 girls every year. It has 24 X 7 CCTV coverage, security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system which ensure the utmost security for girls.

Other facilities like Wi-Fi, fully equipped gym, indoor games hall, study hall ensure the utmost comfort for the students.This block’s entrance is facing north and all the rooms are fully furnished with attached bathrooms with cold and hot water facilities.Both A.C and Non A.C rooms are available for allotment.

BLOCK B

This block has Ground plus 5 floors. This fully furnished hostel provides a comfortable stay, with a safe and a conducive environment for the girl students. For the safety of girls, it is equipped with 24 * 7 surveillance cameras, security guards and biometric attendance. This hostel provides WiFi, power backup and purified water facility. Students can spend their leisure time in the TV hall. Eatery shops, Photocopier service are also available near the block. The block can house above 400 students.

BLOCK C

Block entrance is facing east and all rooms are provided with study tables and storage lockers. Cold and hot water facilities, 24 x 7 Wi-fi facilities, indoor and outdoor games, jogging and walking tracks are also available. photocopier service are present near the premises. It has security guards surveillance, power backup and biometric attendance system which ensure the utmost security for girls.

BLOCK D

Clinic room is available to ensure the medical facilities for the students. Block entrance is facing east and all rooms are provided with study tables and storage lockers. Cold and hot water facilities, 24 x 7 Wi-fi facilities, indoor and outdoor games, jogging and walking tracks are also available.

RULES FOR GIRLS

(Additional to general rules and regulations)

  • Modesty in dress is expected from students
  • Students should bepresent in the hostel between 7.00 pm & 6.00 am. If any student wantsto go out to the city / town they should submit a letter from their parents to the deputy warden indicating the date on which they want to go with time of leaving the hostel and expected time of return. They should also write their names in the register intended for the purpose, giving time of leaving & expectedtime of return. Violation of this rule will result in expulsion from the hostel
  • Roll call will be taken at 07.30 pm daily.
  • Guest is not permitted to stay overnight in the hostel
  • Students will be allowed to go home only if there is a written request for the same from self and parent /guardian. Going home frequently is not permitted
